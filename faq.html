Help · FAQ

The fine print.

Everything you need to know about ordering, shipping, sizing, and what makes Twisted by NG Divine one-of-one.

How long does shipping take?

5–7 business days. Flat $6 shipping on every order. Tracking is sent the moment your twist leaves the studio.

Are all earrings handmade?

Yes. Every pair is hand-braided in Natasha's studio. There are no molds, no production lines, no mass manufacturing.

Are all earrings identical?

No. No two twists are exactly alike. Color placement, braid tension, and finish vary slightly from pair to pair — that's the signature.

Do you accept returns?

No returns on handmade items. Damage claims must be submitted within 14 days of delivery to twistedbyngdivine@gmail.com with a photo of the damage.

What comes with my order?

Every order arrives with the earrings, a dust pouch, and a Confidence Card hand-signed by Natasha.

What sizes are available?

Three sizes: Signature Twist 2" ($20), Bold Twist 3" ($25), Main Character Twist 4" ($30). Pick by the energy you want to give that day.

Are the hoops heavy?

No. Every hoop is built on a fabric-covered elastic foundation, so even the 4" Main Character size stays lightweight on the ear.

How do I care for my Twists?

Keep them dry, store flat in the dust pouch, and avoid perfume or lotion directly on the fabric. Treated well, they last a long time.

Can I request a custom color story?

Sometimes — drop Natasha a note at twistedbyngdivine@gmail.com or DM @nubiancolourgoddess with what you have in mind.

Where do you ship?

Currently shipping within the US. International requests handled case-by-case — email for a quote.

Still have questions?

Just ask.

Email Natasha directly at twistedbyngdivine@gmail.com — you'll get an actual reply from an actual person.
