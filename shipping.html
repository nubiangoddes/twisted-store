Shipping & Returns.

Straight, simple, no surprises. Here's exactly how it works.

Shipping

Flat $6 shipping on every order, every size, every collection. No free-shipping threshold — the price is the price.

Orders ship in 5–7 business days. Because every pair is hand-braided in small batches, the lead time keeps the quality honest.

Tracking is emailed automatically the moment your twist leaves the studio.

Returns

No returns on handmade items.

Every pair is hand-braided, one-of-one, and made to order. Returns aren't possible — but damage claims are.

Damage Claims

Damage claims must be submitted within 14 days of delivery. Email twistedbyngdivine@gmail.com with:

  • Your order number
  • A clear photo of the damage
  • A short description of what happened

You'll hear back from Natasha personally within a couple of business days.

Care

Keep your Twists dry, store them flat in the included dust pouch, and avoid perfume, lotion, or hairspray directly on the fabric. Treated well, they last.
