The Founder

Meet Natasha.

Founder, designer, and hand of every twist. Twisted by NG Divine is a one-woman studio built on color, confidence, and craftsmanship.

Hi, I'm

Natasha Dyson.

I'm the founder of Twisted by NG Divine.

What started as a creative way to transform everyday materials into wearable art grew into a collection of bold braided hoops inspired by color, candy, confidence, and self-expression.

Every earring begins as a fabric-covered elastic ponytail holder. I hand-braid each one in my studio, one pair at a time. There are no molds, no production lines, no two pairs exactly alike.

I make these for the person who walks in last and is remembered first. The person who says color out loud. The person who knows that the loudest thing in the room is also the most personal.

— Natasha Dyson
Instagram · @nubiancolourgoddess TikTok · @natashadyson6
"Twisted. Bold. Divine. — Designed to bend the rules."

The Process

Every piece begins with a single fabric-covered elastic. From there it's hand-braided, shaped, sealed, and finished. The whole process takes time — and that's the point. These are not jewelry-store hoops. These are wearable art objects, made one at a time.

The Promise

  • Hand-braided in small batches.
  • Lightweight, statement-sized hoops only.
  • No two pairs exactly alike.
  • A Confidence Card in every order.
