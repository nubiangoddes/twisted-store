✦ Founder · Designer · Hand-braider

The woman who turned ponytail holders into statement earrings.

Hi, I'm Natasha Dyson — founder of Twisted by NG Divine.

What started as a creative way to transform everyday materials into wearable art grew into a collection of bold braided hoops inspired by color, candy, confidence, and self-expression.

Every pair is hand-braided in my studio. One braid at a time. No molds, no mass production, no two pairs exactly alike — and that's the whole point.

— Natasha Dyson
